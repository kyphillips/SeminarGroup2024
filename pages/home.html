About Us

Welcome to Wayne Summer Camp, hosted by Wayne State College! Our camps offer an exciting array of opportunities for students to explore their passions and interests in a variety of professional fields. From our enriching Summer Music Camp to immersive experiences in athletics, nursing, filmmaking, crime scene investigation, engineering, and beyond, there's something for everyone. At Wayne Summer Camp, we provide affordable and accessible programs designed to inspire and educate students of all ages. Our experienced instructors and hands-on activities ensure that participants gain valuable skills and experiences while having fun and making new friends. Whether you're dreaming of a career in healthcare, the arts, STEM fields, or beyond, Wayne Summer Camp has something to offer. Come join us for an unforgettable summer of learning, creativity, and exploration!
